Axis Bank Young Banker's Program (ABYB)

The Axis Bank Young Bankers program in association with NIIT University, Neemrana Rajasthan, is an initiative to upskill young graduates to lay a foundation for a successful career in banking. On successful completion of the one-year residential program, students are awarded a Post Graduate Diploma in Banking Services along with an assured job opportunity with Axis Bank.

Program

Structure

4 Months Residential Training @ NIIT University
  • Banking Environment
  • Communication for Bankers
  • Customer Service
  • Customer Relationship Management
  • Banking Products & its processes
  • Data-driven Decision Making
  • Marketing for Bankers
  • Banking Operations for Retail Consumers
  • Selling Skills
  • Financial Planning
  • Finacle Workshop
3 Months Internship @ Axis Bank Branch
  • Advanced Business Communication (Online)
  • Learning through observation & shadowing
  • Structured internship diary
  • Hand holding at branches
  • Mentorship by NIIT faculties
5 months On the Job Training @ Axis Bank Branch
  • Banking Technology (Online)
  • Team support
  • Mentoring by seniors
  • Business Goals to help align to business expectations

On successful completion of the first 7 months duration, the candidate gets absorbed at Axis Bank as Assistant manager

Post Graduate Diploma

On successful completion of the program 1 year Post Graduate Diploma in Banking Services certificate from NIIT University.
